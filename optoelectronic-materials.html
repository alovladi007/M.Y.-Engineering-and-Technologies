Advanced Material Solutions

Our optoelectronic materials division specializes in the development and fabrication of cutting-edge III-V semiconductors and quantum dot technologies. We leverage decades of expertise in materials science to deliver solutions that push the boundaries of optical and electronic performance.

Material Systems

  • • Gallium Nitride (GaN) for high-power, high-frequency applications
  • • Indium Phosphide (InP) for photonic integrated circuits
  • • Aluminum Nitride (AlN) for UV optoelectronics
  • • Quantum dots for single-photon sources and displays
  • • Heterostructures for advanced device architectures

Key Applications

  • • High-efficiency LEDs and laser diodes
  • • Photonic integrated circuits (PICs)
  • • Solar cells and photodetectors
  • • Quantum computing components
  • • RF and microwave devices

Technical Capabilities

Epitaxial Growth

MOCVD and MBE systems for precise layer-by-layer growth of III-V materials with atomic-level control

Quantum Dot Synthesis

Colloidal and epitaxial quantum dot fabrication with precise size and composition control

Characterization

Comprehensive optical, electrical, and structural characterization including PL, XRD, AFM, and Hall measurements
