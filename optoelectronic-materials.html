Material Properties & Capabilities

Gallium Nitride (GaN)

Bandgap: 3.4 eV
Breakdown Field: 3.3 MV/cm
Electron Mobility: 1500 cm²/V·s
Thermal Conductivity: 2.3 W/cm·K

Applications: Power electronics, RF devices, LEDs, laser diodes

Indium Phosphide (InP)

Bandgap: 1.34 eV
Electron Mobility: 5400 cm²/V·s
Hole Mobility: 200 cm²/V·s
Dielectric Constant: 12.5

Applications: Photonic ICs, high-speed electronics, photodetectors

Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

Bandgap: 6.2 eV
Breakdown Field: 12 MV/cm
Thermal Conductivity: 3.2 W/cm·K
Piezoelectric Coeff: 5.1 pm/V

Applications: UV LEDs, acoustic devices, high-temperature electronics
