Material Systems

GaN

Gallium Nitride

  • • Wide bandgap (3.4 eV)
  • • High electron mobility
  • • Blue/UV LEDs & lasers
  • • High-power RF devices
InP

Indium Phosphide

  • • Direct bandgap (1.35 eV)
  • • Superior electron velocity
  • • Telecom wavelengths
  • • High-speed electronics
AlN

Aluminum Nitride

  • • Ultra-wide bandgap (6.2 eV)
  • • High thermal conductivity
  • • Deep UV applications
  • • Piezoelectric properties
QD

Quantum Dots

  • • Size-tunable emission
  • • High quantum yield
  • • Single photon sources
  • • Quantum computing
