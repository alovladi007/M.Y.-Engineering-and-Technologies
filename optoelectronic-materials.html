Optoelectronic Materials

Advancing III-V semiconductors, quantum dots, and next-generation photonic materials for revolutionary optical and electronic applications

Technology Overview

Our optoelectronic materials division specializes in the development and optimization of advanced semiconductor materials that bridge the gap between photonics and electronics. We focus on III-V compound semiconductors including Gallium Nitride (GaN), Indium Phosphide (InP), and Aluminum Nitride (AlN), as well as cutting-edge quantum dot technologies.

These materials enable breakthrough applications in high-efficiency LEDs, laser diodes, solar cells, photodetectors, and quantum computing components. Our expertise spans from fundamental material science to device integration and system-level optimization.
