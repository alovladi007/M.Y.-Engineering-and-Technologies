Optoelectronic Materials

Advanced III-V semiconductors, quantum dots, and novel materials for next-generation photonic and optoelectronic devices

Material Systems Overview

Gallium Nitride (GaN)

  • • Wide bandgap (3.4 eV)
  • • High electron mobility
  • • UV/blue LEDs & lasers
  • • High-power RF devices

Indium Phosphide (InP)

  • • Direct bandgap (1.34 eV)
  • • Superior electron velocity
  • • Telecom wavelengths
  • • High-speed photonics

Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

  • • Ultra-wide bandgap (6.2 eV)
  • • Deep UV applications
  • • Piezoelectric properties
  • • High thermal conductivity

Quantum Dots

  • • Size-tunable emission
  • • High quantum yield
  • • Narrow linewidth
  • • Single-photon sources
