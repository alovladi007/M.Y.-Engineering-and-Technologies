Optoelectronic Materials

Advanced III-V semiconductors, GaN, InP, AlN, and quantum dot devices for next-generation photonic and electronic applications

Material Systems Overview

Gallium Nitride (GaN)

  • • Wide bandgap (3.4 eV)
  • • High electron mobility
  • • UV-blue LEDs/lasers
  • • Power electronics

Indium Phosphide (InP)

  • • Direct bandgap (1.35 eV)
  • • High electron velocity
  • • Telecom lasers
  • • High-speed electronics

Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

  • • Ultra-wide bandgap (6.2 eV)
  • • High thermal conductivity
  • • Deep UV emitters
  • • Acoustic devices

Quantum Dots

  • • Size-tunable emission
  • • High quantum yield
  • • Display technology
  • • Single photon sources
