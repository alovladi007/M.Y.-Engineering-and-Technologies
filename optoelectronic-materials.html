Technology Overview

Our optoelectronic materials platform leverages cutting-edge III-V semiconductor technology to create high-performance photonic devices. By combining advanced epitaxial growth techniques with precise material engineering, we develop materials systems that push the boundaries of optical and electronic performance.

From ultraviolet to infrared wavelengths, our materials enable breakthrough applications in communications, sensing, quantum computing, and energy conversion.

Core Material Systems

Gallium Nitride (GaN)

  • • Wide bandgap (3.4 eV)
  • • High electron mobility
  • • UV/blue light emission
  • • High-power RF devices
  • • Power electronics

Indium Phosphide (InP)

  • • Direct bandgap (1.34 eV)
  • • High electron velocity
  • • Telecom wavelengths
  • • Photonic integration
  • • High-speed electronics

Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

  • • Ultra-wide bandgap (6.2 eV)
  • • Deep UV emission
  • • High thermal conductivity
  • • Piezoelectric properties
  • • RF filters & resonators

Quantum Dots

  • • Size-tunable emission
  • • High quantum yield
  • • Single photon sources
  • • Quantum computing
  • • Advanced displays
