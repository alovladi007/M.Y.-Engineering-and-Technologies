Technology Overview

O.R.I.O.N's optoelectronic materials division specializes in the development and fabrication of advanced III-V semiconductor materials and devices. Our expertise spans from fundamental material growth to complex heterostructure engineering, enabling breakthrough applications in photonics, power electronics, and quantum technologies.

We leverage state-of-the-art epitaxy techniques including MOCVD and MBE to create materials with unprecedented control over composition, doping, and interface quality. Our materials enable devices operating from the UV to the far-infrared spectrum, with applications ranging from high-efficiency LEDs to quantum cascade lasers.

Materials Portfolio

Gallium Nitride (GaN)

  • • Wide bandgap (3.4 eV)
  • • High electron mobility
  • • Excellent thermal stability
  • • Blue/UV LEDs & lasers
  • • RF power amplifiers
  • • Power switching devices

Indium Phosphide (InP)

  • • Direct bandgap (1.34 eV)
  • • Superior electron velocity
  • • Low noise characteristics
  • • Telecom lasers (1.3-1.55 μm)
  • • High-speed photodetectors
  • • MMICs for 5G/6G

Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

  • • Ultra-wide bandgap (6.2 eV)
  • • High thermal conductivity
  • • Piezoelectric properties
  • • Deep UV LEDs
  • • SAW/BAW filters
  • • High-temperature electronics

Quantum Dots

  • • Size-tunable emission
  • • High quantum yield
  • • Narrow linewidth
  • • Single photon sources
  • • Quantum computing qubits
  • • Bio-imaging markers
