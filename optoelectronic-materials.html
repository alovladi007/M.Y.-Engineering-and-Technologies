Optoelectronic Materials

Advanced III-V semiconductors, GaN, InP, AlN, and quantum dot devices for next-generation photonic and electronic applications.

Material Portfolio

III-V Semiconductors

Gallium Nitride (GaN)

  • • Wide bandgap (3.4 eV) for high-power applications
  • • Blue/UV LEDs and laser diodes
  • • High-frequency power amplifiers
  • • Power electronics (600V-1200V devices)

Indium Phosphide (InP)

  • • Direct bandgap (1.35 eV) for telecom wavelengths
  • • High-speed photodetectors
  • • Laser diodes (1.3-1.55 μm)
  • • Photonic integrated circuits

Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

  • • Ultra-wide bandgap (6.2 eV)
  • • Deep UV LEDs (200-280 nm)
  • • High-temperature electronics
  • • Acoustic wave devices

Quantum Dot Technologies

Material Systems

  • • CdSe/ZnS core-shell structures
  • • InP/ZnS for Cd-free applications
  • • PbS for NIR applications
  • • Perovskite quantum dots

Applications

  • • QLED displays with >90% color gamut
  • • Quantum dot solar cells
  • • Biomedical imaging markers
  • • Single-photon sources for quantum computing

Key Properties

  • • Size-tunable emission (400-1600 nm)
  • • Narrow emission bandwidth (<30 nm FWHM)
  • • High quantum yield (>95%)
  • • Solution processability

Device Integration

  • • Quantum dot film deposition
