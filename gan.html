Gallium Nitride (GaN)

High-frequency, high-power semiconductor technology enabling superior efficiency in power electronics, RF devices, and optoelectronics.

Material Properties

  • •
    Wide Bandgap: 3.4 eV enabling high breakdown voltage
  • •
    High Electron Mobility: 2000 cm²/V·s for fast switching
  • •
    High Critical Field: 3.3 MV/cm for high power density
  • •
    Thermal Conductivity: 1.3 W/cm·K for better heat dissipation
  • •
    High Temperature Operation: Stable up to 300°C

Key Advantages

  • ✓
    Higher Efficiency: Reduced switching and conduction losses
  • ✓
    Smaller Size: 10x power density vs silicon devices
  • ✓
    Faster Switching: MHz operation capability
  • ✓
    Lower System Cost: Reduced cooling and passive components
  • ✓
    Radiation Hardness: Suitable for space applications
