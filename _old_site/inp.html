Indium Phosphide (InP)

Ultra-high-speed optoelectronic devices and photonic integrated circuits for next-generation optical communications and sensing.

Material Properties

  • • Direct bandgap: 1.35 eV at 300K
  • • High electron mobility: 5400 cm²/V·s
  • • High saturation velocity: 2×10⁷ cm/s
  • • Low noise characteristics
  • • Excellent radiation resistance

Applications

  • • High-speed photodetectors
  • • Laser diodes (1.3-1.55 μm)
  • • Photonic integrated circuits
  • • Millimeter-wave devices
  • • Solar cells for space
