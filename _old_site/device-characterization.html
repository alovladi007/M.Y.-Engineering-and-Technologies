Characterization Services

High-Frequency Analysis

  • • S-parameter measurements up to 110 GHz

  • • Load-pull and source-pull testing
  • • Noise figure measurements
  • • Large-signal characterization

Thermal Analysis

  • • IR thermal imaging with 5μm resolution
  • • Transient thermal measurements
  • • Junction temperature mapping

  • • ESD testing (HBM, MM, CDM)
  • • Burn-in testing capabilities

Failure Analysis

  • • FIB (Focused Ion Beam) cross-sectioning
  • • TEM (Transmission Electron Microscopy)
  • • SEM with EDS capabilities
  • • Decapsulation and delayering
  • • Root cause analysis reporting
