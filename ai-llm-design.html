Platform Overview

Our AI/LLM-Assisted Design platform represents a paradigm shift in materials discovery and device optimization. By combining state-of-the-art language models with domain-specific knowledge graphs and predictive models, we enable researchers to accelerate the development cycle from years to weeks.

The platform integrates literature mining, property prediction, hypothesis generation, and inverse design capabilities into a unified system that learns from both published research and experimental data.

Key Achievement:

Building on ITMO's breakthrough showing GPT-4 can predict nanoparticle shapes with ~81% accuracy, our platform extends this capability across the entire materials design workflow.

Data Schemas

material_entity.yaml

material:
  name: "Au nanorod"
  composition: "Au"
  structure: 
    type: "nanorod"
    aspect_ratio: 3.6
  ligands: ["CTAB", "Ag+"]
  target_properties:
    - name: "LSPR_longitudinal_nm"
      value: 800
      tol: 20

synthesis_step.yaml

step:
  method: "seed-mediated growth"
  reagents:
    - {name: "HAuCl4", conc_mM: 0.5}
    - {name: "CTAB", conc_mM: 100}
    - {name: "AgNO3", conc_mM: 0.1}
    - {name: "ascorbic acid", conc_mM: 0.64}
  conditions: 
    T_C: 28
    pH: 2.5
    order: ["CTAB","HAuCl4","AgNO3","AA","seed"]
    time_min: 30
  characterization: ["UV-Vis", "TEM"]
  outcomes: 
    morphology: "rod"
    AR: 3.5-3.8
    LSPR_nm: 780-820
