Technology Overview

O.R.I.O.N's Advanced Power Electronics division is at the forefront of developing ultra-high efficiency power conversion systems using wide-bandgap semiconductors. Our GaN and SiC-based solutions achieve unprecedented power densities and efficiencies, enabling the electrification of transportation, renewable energy integration, and next-generation data center power architectures.

We combine advanced materials science with innovative circuit topologies and AI-driven control algorithms to create power systems that are not just more efficient, but fundamentally smarter and more adaptable to dynamic load conditions and grid requirements.
