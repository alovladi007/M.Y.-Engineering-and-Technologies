Technology Overview

Our Advanced Power Electronics division pioneers the development of ultra-efficient power conversion and management systems using wide-bandgap semiconductors. We specialize in Gallium Nitride (GaN) and Silicon Carbide (SiC) based solutions that deliver unprecedented efficiency, power density, and reliability.

From high-frequency inverters achieving >99% efficiency to revolutionary solid-state transformers and wireless power transfer systems, our technologies are enabling the electrification of transportation, renewable energy integration, and next-generation data centers.
