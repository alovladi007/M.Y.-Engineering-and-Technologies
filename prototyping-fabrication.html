Fabrication Services

Epitaxy Services

Growth Techniques

  • • MOCVD for III-V materials (GaN, InP, AlGaAs)
  • • MBE for quantum structures and superlattices
  • • HVPE for thick layers and templates
  • • ALD for ultra-thin films and interfaces

Capabilities

  • • Layer thickness: 1 nm to 100 μm
  • • Wafer sizes: 2" to 6" diameter
  • • In-situ monitoring (RHEED, pyrometry)
  • • Custom heterostructure design

Wafer Processing

Process Capabilities

  • • Photolithography (365nm to 193nm DUV)
  • • E-beam lithography for sub-100nm features
  • • Dry etching (ICP-RIE, RIE, IBE)
  • • Wet chemical processing

Metallization

  • • E-beam evaporation (multi-source)
  • • Sputter deposition (DC/RF)
  • • Electroplating for thick metals
  • • Ohmic and Schottky contacts

Nano-fabrication

Advanced Techniques

  • • Nanoimprint lithography (NIL)
  • • Focused ion beam (FIB) milling
  • • Self-assembled monolayers
  • • Block copolymer lithography

Applications

  • • Photonic crystals and metamaterials
  • • Quantum dots and nanowires
  • • Plasmonic nanostructures
  • • MEMS/NEMS devices
