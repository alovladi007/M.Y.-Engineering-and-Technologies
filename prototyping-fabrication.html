Service Overview

Our prototyping and fabrication services provide researchers and companies with access to state-of-the-art facilities and expertise for developing next-generation devices. From initial concept to functional prototype, we offer comprehensive support throughout the development cycle.

With advanced epitaxial growth systems, cutting-edge nanofabrication tools, and experienced process engineers, we accelerate the path from laboratory discovery to commercial reality.
