Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with BIOMEDICAL?

Sign up for an account, complete the onboarding process, and start by uploading your first medical images or connecting your biosensing devices.

What file formats are supported?

We support DICOM, JPEG, PNG, and TIFF for medical images, plus various biosensor data formats including CSV and JSON.

How accurate are the AI diagnostics?

Our AI models achieve 99.7% accuracy in medical imaging analysis, with confidence scores and explainable AI features for clinical decision support.

Can I integrate with my existing EHR?

Yes, we offer comprehensive integration with major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, and Allscripts through our API and FHIR compliance.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, and comprehensive audit logging to protect your data.

What support options are available?

We offer email support, phone support, live chat, and dedicated account managers based on your plan level.

Can I customize the platform?

Yes, we offer extensive customization options including custom workflows, branded interfaces, and tailored AI models for your specific needs.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via email at support@biomedical.com, phone at +1 (555) 012-3456, or through the live chat feature in your dashboard.
