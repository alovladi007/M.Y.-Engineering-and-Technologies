Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform

How does HIPAA compliance work?

Our platform is fully HIPAA compliant with end-to-end encryption, audit logging, and BAA support for healthcare organizations.

What types of medical data can you process?

We support medical imaging (DICOM), biosensor data, clinical records, and real-time monitoring data with full regulatory compliance.

Yes, we provide comprehensive integration support for PACS, EHR, DICOM, and FHIR systems with dedicated technical assistance.
