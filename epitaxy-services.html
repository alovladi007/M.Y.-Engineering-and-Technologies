Our Epitaxy Capabilities

MOCVD Growth

Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition for high-quality III-V semiconductor layers

  • •
    Materials: GaN, AlGaN, InGaN, GaAs, InP, AlGaAs
  • •
    Substrates: Sapphire, SiC, Si, GaAs, InP
  • •
    Thickness control: ±1% uniformity across 6" wafers
  • •
    Doping range: 10¹⁵ to 10²⁰ cm⁻³

MBE Growth

Molecular Beam Epitaxy for ultra-precise quantum structures and research applications

  • •
    Precision: Monolayer control capability
  • •
    Applications: Quantum wells, dots, and wires
  • •
    In-situ monitoring: RHEED for real-time analysis
  • •
    Ultra-high vacuum: Base pressure < 10⁻¹⁰ Torr
