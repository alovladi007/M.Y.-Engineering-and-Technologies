Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GAIA pricing

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial for the Professional plan. No credit card required to get started.

What happens if I exceed my node limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan or purchase additional node capacity.

Do you offer custom pricing?

Yes, our Enterprise plan includes custom pricing based on your specific needs and infrastructure requirements.
