Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about GAIA

How quickly can I see energy savings?

Most customers see immediate energy savings within 24 hours of deployment, with full optimization benefits realized within 2-4 weeks.

What's the typical ROI for GAIA?

Customers typically see ROI within 6-12 months, with average energy cost savings of 15-30% and payback periods of 8-18 months.

Do you offer on-premises deployment?

Yes, GAIA supports both cloud and on-premises deployments, with hybrid options available for organizations with specific compliance requirements.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 technical support, dedicated customer success managers, comprehensive documentation, and training programs for your team.
