Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about GAIA

How much energy savings can I expect with GAIA?

Most organizations see 15-30% energy reduction within the first month of deployment. The exact savings depend on your workload patterns and current optimization level.

Will GAIA affect my application performance?

GAIA is designed to maintain your SLAs while optimizing energy consumption. Our PID controllers ensure latency impact is typically less than 5%.

What Kubernetes versions are supported?

GAIA supports Kubernetes 1.24 and above. We test with the latest stable releases and provide compatibility updates for new versions.

How does the green window scheduling work?

Green windows automatically adjust power caps and batch sizes during low-carbon energy periods (typically overnight) to maximize efficiency while maintaining performance.

Can I customize optimization policies?

Yes! GAIA provides flexible EnergyPolicy CRDs that allow you to set custom power caps, latency targets, and optimization parameters for different workloads.
