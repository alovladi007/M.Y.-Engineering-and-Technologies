Aluminum Nitride (AlN)

Ultra-wide bandgap semiconductor for extreme environment applications, high-power electronics, and deep UV optoelectronics.

Material Properties

  • • Ultra-wide bandgap: 6.2 eV
  • • High thermal conductivity: 285 W/m·K
  • • High breakdown field: 15 MV/cm
  • • Piezoelectric properties
  • • Chemical stability

Applications

  • • Deep UV LEDs (200-280 nm)
  • • High-temperature electronics
  • • RF filters and resonators
  • • Power electronic substrates
  • • Acoustic wave devices
