Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

We specialize in advanced photonics, cybersecurity, hardware design, biomedical engineering, geospatial technology, and nanotechnology projects. Our expertise spans from research and development to commercial product implementation.

Do you offer consulting services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical consulting services across all our technology domains. Our experts can help with system design, optimization, troubleshooting, and strategic technology planning.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple consulting projects may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive R&D projects can span 6-18 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We serve clients globally and have experience working across different time zones and regulatory environments. We can accommodate various communication preferences and project requirements.
